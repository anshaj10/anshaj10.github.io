Transparency & Trust

Our Code of Conduct

We operate with full transparency, integrity, and respect for every student. Here's our commitment to you.

1

Honest Counselling: We provide truthful, unbiased guidance about courses, universities, and visa prospects — with no false promises or misleading claims.

2

Data Privacy: All personal information shared by students is strictly confidential and never shared with third parties without consent.

3

No Hidden Charges: All fees and charges are communicated clearly upfront. We do not levy any hidden or surprise costs at any stage.

4

Ethical Documentation: We strictly prohibit falsification of any academic or personal documents and will not assist in any fraudulent application.

5

Respectful Service: Every student is treated with dignity and respect, regardless of academic background, gender, caste, or religion.

6

Timely Communication: We commit to responding to all queries within 24 hours and keeping students updated at every stage of their application.

7

Student-First Approach: Our recommendations are always in the best interest of the student's career and future, not driven by commissions or partnerships.
